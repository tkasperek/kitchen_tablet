# kitchen_tablet

## How to use old tablet as kitchen tablet or family hub.

### Goal:
affordable device for kitchen that synchronize with residents smartphones

### Use cases:
- shopping list,
- kitchen timer and digital assistant,
- recipes - storing and reading,
- house todo list,
- house calendar,
- information board,
- photo frame.

## Hardware:
- tablet compatible with below software:
   Nexus 10 in my case,
- tablet charger,
- tablet wall mount:
   https://www.amazon.com/Universal-Tablet-Holder-Surface-Brainwavz/dp/B07Y84F85V

## Software:
- Trello app:
   https://trello.com/
- Digital assistant: Siri / Google Assistant / Alexa / Cortana

## Tips:
- you can use tablet with broken battery
